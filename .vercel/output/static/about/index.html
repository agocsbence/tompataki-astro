About

Tom is an Art Director, Production Designer, and Product Designer.

Born in Hungary in 1986, he took an early interest in art and design.
“It all started with my grandfather, who was a carpenter. I think I was about 5, and I wanted a one-meter-high pirate ship for my toys.
He just asked me to draw a sketch, and the next morning the ship was on the table.
From that moment on, a pencil and a piece of paper have always been in my pocket.”

Right before graduating high school, he made it to the finals of the National Secondary School Academic Competition in Art History. He received acceptance offers from nearly every major university in the country and chose Architecture as a natural progression of his passion for design.
He studied Architecture at the University of Pécs (HUN) and at IUAV in Venice (IT). During his final semester, he decided to switch to movies, left university, and moved to Budapest.

He started working with Emmy-nominated Production Designer Judit Varga.

“She was a really good mentor. I had no idea how this whole industry worked, but I was eager to be a part of it. I brought my passion for movies, and she gave me the knowledge.”

He began his career as a Set Decorator and assistant Art Director, gaining experience across a wide range of film and commercial productions.
His passion and success have allowed him to earn recognition as a rising Art Director and Production Designer in the industry.

            “It’s just like a big playground…and I love to play”
