The Hollywood Lounge is a really old project. The first sketches made more than 8 years ago.
We wanted to create a chair what constitutes ultimate and luxurious relaxation, wherein the user’s position evokes
the boundary of dreams and real life.
Although the appearance of the Hollywood Lounge Chair seems rather futuristic, the basic idea came from the 60s.
The first hint was inspired by forms of Sheats-Goldstein residence by John Lautner.
The second thought came from Hans J. Wegner’s Flag Halyard Chair.
The quintessence of these two designs mixed with a posh pool parties’ feeling helped to
come up with the right final form.

Now we are working on the prototype. The first Hollywood Lounge comes out in September 2018
